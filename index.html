Rainbow Poem

A rainbow arcs across the sky,
A painted smile where colors lie.
Red and orange, yellow too,
Green and blue, and violet hue.

It comes after the rain’s embrace,
A fleeting bridge of light and grace.
A promise bright, a gentle show,
That even storms bring beauty's glow.

Across the sky a bow unfurls,
In vibrant hues, a wonder swirls.
**Red** ignites the morning light,
**Orange** warms the fading night.

**Yellow** glows with cheerful might,
**Green** is calm and softly bright.
**Blue** descends like ocean's deep,
**Indigo** in twilight’s sweep.

At last, **Violet** graces high,
A grand display from earth to sky.
A spectrum wide, a joy to see,
A rainbow's gift to you and me. nareshIT
